Nutrient Powder Recipe

Combine healthy fats, proteins, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals in one delicious powder!

Ingredients Breakdown

Creating a nutrient powder with roasted peanuts, walnuts, almonds, cashews, jaggery, and powdered rice provides a balanced mix of healthy fats, proteins, carbohydrates, and essential nutrients.

Making Process

1. Roast the rice until golden and fragrant.

2. Blend roasted rice, peanuts, walnuts, almonds, cashews, and jaggery into a fine powder.

3. Mix all ingredients thoroughly to ensure even distribution.

4. Store in an airtight container to keep fresh.

Suggested Use

  • Smoothies: Add a tablespoon to your smoothies for an energy boost.
  • Breakfast Cereal: Sprinkle over oatmeal or yogurt.
  • Baking: Incorporate into muffins, pancakes, or bread.
  • Snack: Mix with water or milk for a quick nutritious snack.
