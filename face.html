Step-by-Step Guide for Preparing Your Homemade Face Spray

Ingredients:

  • Aloe Vera Gel – 2 tablespoons
  • Fresh Carrot Juice – 1 tablespoon
  • Vitamin E Oil – 2-3 drops
  • Geogard ECT – 0.6 ml for 100 ml of spray
  • Distilled Water – 2 tablespoons
  • Essential Oils (optional) – 2-3 drops each
  • Spray Bottle – 50-100 ml

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Fresh Carrot Juice: Peel and wash a small carrot. Blend or juice until smooth, strain if needed. Measure 1 tablespoon of juice.
  2. Prepare Aloe Vera Gel: Extract fresh aloe vera gel and measure 2 tablespoons.
  3. Mix Aloe Vera and Carrot Juice: Combine and stir.
  4. Add Vitamin E Oil: Add 2-3 drops from a capsule.
  5. Add Geogard ECT: Add 0.6 ml and mix well.
  6. Optional – Add Essential Oils: Add 2-3 drops of tea tree or lavender oil and stir.
  7. Dilute with Distilled Water: Add 2 tablespoons of distilled water.
  8. Transfer to Spray Bottle: Pour into sterilized spray bottle using a funnel.
  9. Shake Well: Shake before each use.

Usage Instructions:

  • Patch Test: Apply a small amount and wait 24 hours.
  • Apply: Spray on clean, dry skin, focusing on acne-prone areas.
  • Frequency: Use in the morning and evening or as needed.

Storage Instructions:

  • Store in a cool, dark place (like a refrigerator).
  • With Geogard ECT, the spray lasts 3-4 weeks; without it, use within 5-7 days.
